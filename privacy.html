Squads — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 6 July 2026

Squads ("the app") is a recreational sports team generator and scoreboard published by Flux Developments ("we", "us"). This policy explains what data the app handles. The short version: your data stays on your phone — we don't collect, store, or transmit any of it.

Data we collect

None. Squads has no user accounts, no analytics, no advertising, and no server. We do not collect, receive, sell, or share any personal data.

Data stored on your device

The app saves the following locally on your device only: your display name, preferred positions, avatar colour, optional Instagram handle, session and tournament data, and match history (scores, XP, badges). This information never leaves your phone unless you explicitly share it (for example, sharing a result card image via the iOS share sheet). Deleting the app, or using "Reset profile & data" in the Profile screen, permanently removes all of it.

Camera

The app requests camera access solely to scan Squads QR codes for joining sessions. Camera frames are processed on-device and are never recorded, stored, or transmitted.

Third parties

The app contains no third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs. If you tap an Instagram handle, you will be taken to Instagram, whose own privacy policy then applies.

Children

Squads does not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children, because it collects no data at all.

Future changes

If we introduce optional accounts or online features in a future version, this policy will be updated before those features launch, and any data handling will be clearly explained in the app.
